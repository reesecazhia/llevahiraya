The Climate Crisis and Disasters

Ano nga ba ang kinalaman ng nangyayaring krisis sa klima sa mga darating pang sakuna?

Because the Philippines is located just above the equator and faces the western Pacific, warm tropical waters fuel typhoons that hit the country. Due to this fact, the country is very susceptible to climate issues, especially climate change and global warming because the hardest typhoons hit the country. But, what exactly is the connection between the disasters we are experiencing and climate change?

More heat in the atmosphere and warmer ocean surface temperatures can lead to increased wind speeds in tropical storms. And recently, scientists have protested for the world to take action so that climate can be restored and change be done so we would not bring upon our own demise. This is because, when the set time limit according to studies has been met and Earth’s temperature hasn't cooled down and no climate action was done, then catastrophic chain reaction of disasters may occur.

Climate change projections show that global average surface warming will increase, sea levels will rise as glaciers melt, hot extremes, heat waves, and heavy precipitation events will become more common, more precipitation at higher latitudes and less precipitation in most subtropical land areas, tropical cyclones (typhoons and hurricanes) will become more intense, with larger peak wind speeds and more heavy rainfall.

Natural hazards do not create disasters; catastrophes come from the interaction of an exposed, vulnerable, and ill-prepared population or community with a hazard event. Climate change will affect disaster risks in two ways: first, by increasing the likelihood of increased weather and climate hazards, and second, by increasing community vulnerability to natural hazards, particularly through ecosystem degradation, reduced water and food availability, and changes in livelihoods. Climate change will add to the stresses of environmental degradation and unplanned urbanization, diminishing communities' ability to deal with even the current levels of weather threats.

So, what can we do?

One may wonder if futile individual efforts for climate action actually have an effect on the overall improvement of the environment’s health. However, even though many people contribute to going green, there is still a significant gap that can only be met by large corporations who are contributing largely to climate change. In fact, it isn’t you that sully the Earth when you eat non-organic food, it was a dot on the large carbon emissions made by companies that continue to burn fossil fuels. Therefore, the real path to climate action is to make them accountable. In this sense, climate activism plays a huge role in calling out the big companies or calling out the government into holding them responsible. Because, the commoners can’t afford to buy expensive sustainable meat, clothes, or necessities. Of course, they would opt for the commercial cheaper choice because they don’t have a choice. Those who can afford to go green are the rich folks. Those who can afford it are those who are contributing the most to climate change and then blaming the consumers for it.

Ano ang kaya nating gawin?

Maaaring nakapagtataka sa isa kung ang walang saysay na pagsisikap ng isang indibidwal para sa pagkilos ng klima ay talagang may epekto sa pangkalahatang pagpapabuti ng kalusugan ng kapaligiran. Gayunpaman, kahit na maraming mga tao ang nakakatutulong sa kaayusan nito, mayroon pa ring isang makabuluhang puwang na maaari lamang matugunan ng mga malalaking korporasyon na higit na nakakaapekto sa pagbabago ng klima. Sa katunayan, hindi ikaw ang direktang dumudungis sa kapaligiran kapag kumakain ka ng hindi organikong pagkain, ito ay isang tuldok lamang sa malaking paglabas ng carbon na ginawa ng mga kumpanya na patuloy na nagsusunog ng mga fossil fuels. Samakatuwid, ang tunay na hakbang sa pagkilos para sa klima ay magkaroon sila ng pananagutan. Sa kahulugan na ito, ang pagkilos ay gumaganap ng isang malaking papel sa pagtawag sa mga malalaking kumpanya o pagtawag sa gobyerno na may pananagutan sa kanila. Sapagkat, hindi kayang bilhin ng mga pangkaraniwang tao ang mamahaling karne, damit, o mga pangangailangan na napapatagal. Mas pipiliin nila ang komersyal na mura dahil wala silang ibang pagpipilian. Ang may mga kakayahang makatulong sa kapaligiran o kalikasan ay ang mayayaman. At kung sino pa ang mga ito ay sila pang mas nakakaambag sa pagbabago ng klima na sumisisi sa mga mamimili ukol dito.

Furthermore, if climate action can be fixed, then disasters wouldn’t be as catastrophic or as unpredictable. It will be something that this website can make you aware of fully. Because right now, awareness is really the rational way to mitigate disaster impacts when it is so unpredictable due to the climate crisis.

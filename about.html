About HIRAYA

HIRAYA is a website made as a material for the Capstone Project of researchers in Luis Palad Integrated High School. Therefore, the target audience of the website are the residents of Tayabas to help them achieve Disaster Awareness.

Some Features of the Website Are:

  • Help Tayabas residents find their evacuation sites
  • Present an adapted Flood Map of Flood Hazard Zones in Tayabas
  • Help the users gain disaster knowledge by learning through a game
  • Learn how to interpret disaster reports and alerts.
  • Learn about the Climate Crisis and its connection with Disasters.
